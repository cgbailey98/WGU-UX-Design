Food and Fun

Entertainment

Taniti has many different activities to offer depending on what you're looking for. Here are a few:

  • Beach relaxation
  • Rainforest exploration
  • Helicopter tours to volcanos and other locations
  • Local history museum
  • Fishing tours
  • Snorkeling
  • Zip-lining
  • Pubs and clubs
  • Sightseeing
If you're interseted in any of these, be sure to book ahead of time as spots go fast!

Restaurants

If you're wanting to eat out and depending on what you're looking for, chances are, Taniti has it covered. There are 10 restaurants on the island with a variety of flavors. You can find American-style cuisine, Pan-Asian cuisine, or if you're feeling adventurous, you can try the local food!

Grocery

If staying in and cooking is more your speed, fret not Taniti has that too! The island offers two supermarkets, two smaller grocery stores, and a 24/7 convenience store. Keep in mind, being on an island may result in higher grocery prices than you're used to but the fresh fruit makes it well worth it!
