Lodging

On the island of Taniti, there are plenty of options for where to stay. The options range from budget friendly hostels all the way up to 4 star resorts with family owned and opperated hotels and bed and breakfasts in between. The options are below.

Hostels

Interested in a more budget friendly option? Booking a hostel may be the best option for you.

Hotels

If you're looking for something a bit nicer but not break the bank, booking a hotel may be a good option.

Bed and Breakfastsg

Wanting a more cozy experience? Try booking with one of the few bed and breakfasts the island has to offer.

Resorts

Wanting to treat yourself and those you're vacationing with? Book with the 4 star resort for your island getaway!
