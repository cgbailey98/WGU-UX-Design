Getting to and around the island

Arriving

Possibly the most important decision you'll make is how to get to Taniti. The most common method is by air. There is a small airport that serves the island but there are plans for it to expand. The other option would be by a cruise ship. This option isn't as quick but it does make travel more enjoyable. Cruise ships arrive to the island weekly.

Transportation on the island

During your stay on Taniti, there's a handful of ways to get arround:

  • Public buses
  • Private buses
  • Taxis
  • Rental cars
  • Rental bikes
  • Walking
